Hello. We're Jude's, a family ice cream company dedicated to crafting unbelievably delicious stripey tubs of joy.

Our dad was starting an ice cream company. What began as a barely whispered idea was soon actually happening. He started small and set to work in our old barn at home in Hampshire. Our mum joined him and he decided to name the company after her. On 19th December 2002 he walked across the field to the village pub and sold the very first tub of Jude’s ice cream. We (Alex and Chow) soon realised that the ice cream was seriously good and were convinced it should be served in the best restaurants. So we joined our parents at Jude's...

FAQs

Will the ice cream be left on the doorstep if the recipient isn’t at home?

Yes, the ice cream is packed with dry ice which keeps it frozen until 6pm on the day of delivery, so it’s not a problem if the person receiving your gift box is not at home.

How long will the ice cream stay cold for if the recipient is not at home to receive it?

The ice cream is packed with dry ice which keeps it cold until 6pm on the day of delivery, so it should still be frozen when they get home.

How much space will the ice cream take up in my freezer?

6 x 460ml tubs of our utterly delicious ice cream takes up approximately as much space as a standard shoe box (not as much as you might think!).

Is your delivery partner eco-friendly too?

We have chosen to work with DPD as they have made a commitment to making their deliveries Carbon Neutral. You can read more about their commitment here.
